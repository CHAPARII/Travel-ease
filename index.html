Popular Destinations

Rajgir Hills

Discover the serene beauty of Rajgir Hills in Bihar—where ancient history, lush trails, and tranquil hot springs create a perfect escape!

Nalanda University

Explore Nalanda, the world's oldest university—where ancient ruins tell tales of wisdom, culture, and a glorious past!

Gol Ghar

Visit Gol Ghar in Patna, a unique granary with panoramic city views and a glimpse into Bihar's rich history!

Darbhanga Darbar

Discover Darbhanga, the cultural heart of Mithila, renowned for its history, palaces, and vibrant traditions!

Valmiki National Park

Experience the wild beauty of Valmiki National Park, Bihar's green gem, home to diverse wildlife and breathtaking landscapes!

Sasaram

Explore Sasaram, home to Sher Shah Suri's majestic tomb, a stunning blend of history and architecture!

Bodh Gaya

Taste Of Bihar

Litti Chokha

Litti Choka, a traditional Bihari delicacy, pairs roasted wheat balls (*litti*) with smoky mashed vegetables (*choka*), offering a flavorful taste of India's rustic cuisine.

Samosa Chat

Samosa Chaat is a flavorful Indian street food dish, combining crispy samosas with tangy chutneys, yogurt, and spices for a deliciously savory experience.

Handi Mutton

Handi Mutton is a rich and aromatic Indian dish, slow-cooked in a clay pot (*handi*) with tender mutton, spices, and a luscious gravy, offering an authentic burst of flavors.
